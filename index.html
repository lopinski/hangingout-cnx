About HangingOut

Tired of scrolling through dozens of chaotic WhatsApp and Facebook groups just to figure out what's going on today? We were too. HangingOut CNX is a free, community-driven tool built for everyone—locals, nomads, and travelers. Our AI automatically curates the best free events across Chiang Mai in one place, so you can stop scrolling and start living.
